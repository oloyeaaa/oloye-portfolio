Here's a stat that should worry every B2B marketer: 81% of companies in our latest benchmark scored below 30 out of 100 on AI visibility.

That means when a buyer asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google's AI Mode to recommend a solution in their category, four out of five companies don't show up at all.

## What Is AI Visibility?

AI visibility measures how well AI platforms can find, understand, and recommend your business. It's not the same as SEO — you can rank on page one of Google and still be completely invisible to AI search.

AI platforms don't just crawl pages. They synthesise information from across the web, looking for entities they can confidently recommend. If your content isn't structured in a way that AI can parse, cite, and attribute, you're invisible.

## Why It Matters Now

The shift is already happening. B2B buyers are increasingly starting their research with AI tools rather than Google. They're asking questions like "What's the best marketing automation platform for mid-market SaaS?" and expecting direct answers.

If your company isn't in those answers, you're losing deals before you even know they exist.

## Four Dimensions of AI Visibility

From our research scanning 150+ B2B companies, we score AI visibility across four dimensions:

1. **Citation Presence** — Does the AI mention your brand when asked about your category?
2. **Entity Recognition** — Does the AI understand what your company does?
3. **Content Structure** — Is your content formatted in a way AI can parse?
4. **Citation Breadth** — How many different AI platforms cite you?

Most companies score well on maybe one of these. The leaders score well on all four.

## What You Can Do

Start by auditing your current AI visibility. Search for your category on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Mode. Are you mentioned? Are your competitors?

Then focus on structured, authoritative content that AI platforms can cite. Original research, clear entity definitions, and well-structured pages that make it easy for AI to understand what you do and why you're credible.

This is the new battleground for B2B visibility. The companies that move now will have a significant advantage.

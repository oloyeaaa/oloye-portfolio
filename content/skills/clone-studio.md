## What it does

Pick a studio set, give it a script, and get back a photoreal, lip-synced talking-head clip, brand-stamped and ready to post. For when you want a face on camera without filming.

## How it works

It runs four modes across two providers, so you trade cost, brand control, and lip-sync quality per clip: a cheap all-in-one mode, a hybrid that builds the studio scene then animates and syncs it, a fix-the-sync mode for an existing clip, and a trained-avatar mode. It carries a studio prompt library so the set looks intentional, not generic.

## When to reach for it

"Make a talking head." "Put me in a podcast studio talking." "Talking-head video from this script."

## The honest bit

Needs your own account on one of the two named providers. Some modes need a one-time setup to mint your own avatar and voice. The cheapest mode runs on the owned stack; the higher-fidelity modes cost provider render minutes.

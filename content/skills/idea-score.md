## What it does

Paste one product idea, and who it is for if you know. You get an honest read in one pass: a score out of 100, the verdict in plain words, and the single biggest risk most likely to sink it. No sugar-coating. If the idea is weak it says so before you spend a month on it.

## How it works

It scores the idea on four things: is the buyer specific, is the pain real and painful, would anyone pay for the fix, and can you actually reach those people. Then it names the one risk that matters most, so you know exactly what to test first.

## When to reach for it

"Score my idea." "Is this worth building?" "Would anyone actually buy this?"

## The honest bit

This is a one-pass read, not a full validation. It scores what you paste; it does not scan live demand, track your killed ideas, or write your pre-sell assets. That deeper version is The Validator, the full research employee this skill is one working piece of.

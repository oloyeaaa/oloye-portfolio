## What it does

Take a framework, a checklist, or any idea you own, and turn it into a short branded PDF a prospect actually wants, plus the caption to promote it and a way to deliver it.

## How it works

You point it at the thing you want to give away. It writes the one-pager, lays it out on brand with your cover photo and wordmark, and hands you the promo caption and the delivery method to go with it.

## When to reach for it

"Give away this." "Make a lead magnet." "Turn this into a freebie, a guide, or a cheat sheet."

## The honest bit

Needs Node for the render step. You supply the brand photo and wordmark; it does the layout and the copy. One clean PDF, ready to send.

## What it does

Turn a still, a logo or a product image, into a short piece of motion: an animated brand promo or a kinetic-graphics ad, built on Higgsfield.

## How it works

You give it the image and the vibe. It builds the motion piece and hands back the finished clip. Pair it with render-room when you need the source asset generated first.

## When to reach for it

"Animate my logo." "Make a motion ad from this." "Turn this product into motion." "Kinetic graphics promo."

## The honest bit

Runs on your own Higgsfield account. Paid renders spend credits, so it confirms the cost before it runs anything that costs money. For the routing between this, render-room and product-ad, the box carries a one-page guide so the three never fight over one job.

## What it does

A voice and copy system for anything public: hooks, captions, scripts, emails, posts. It locks the target first (who it is for, one emotion, one action), rides a proven framework, and runs a 12-point human check that strips the AI tells before the draft ever reaches you.

## How it works

You route a draft through it. It kills the jargon and the filler, leads with the number, keeps one call to action, and refuses the tics that make copy read like a model wrote it: the rule-of-three padding, the fake candour, the signposting. What comes back reads plain and human.

## When to reach for it

Any time you are writing or polishing something that goes out in public and needs to sound like you.

## The honest bit

It ships calibrated to one voice on purpose, so you can see it working from the first run. Making it write like you is a short, documented setup (a quick calibration), not a one-click switch. The 12-point checklist inside is the same one used on every draft.

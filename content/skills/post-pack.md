## What it does

Turn one idea, or a quick voice note, into seven ready-to-post captions with hashtags, all in your voice. It is the part you run on day one, before any of the heavier tools.

## How it works

You give it a single idea. It writes seven distinct posts around it, runs each through the voice check, and hands them back finished. No blank page, no staring at the calendar.

## When to reach for it

"A week of posts." "Write my social posts." "I don't know what to post." "Fill my content calendar."

## The honest bit

Works standalone with nothing extra to configure. If you want, it can schedule straight to your channels, or just hand you a plain schedule table to post by hand.

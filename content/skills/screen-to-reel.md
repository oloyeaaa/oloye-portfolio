## What it does

Point it at a raw screen recording, or any landscape clip, and get back a post-ready 9:16 vertical: readable branded captions, a scroll-pan reveal, and a call-to-action end card. Made for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, and WhatsApp Status.

## How it works

It reframes the clip to vertical, burns in captions on a solid bar with your accent, builds the reveal, appends the end card, and stitches it together with crossfades. It also hands you the caption, the hashtags, and cover guidance, so the piece is ready to post, not just ready to edit.

## When to reach for it

"Turn this recording into a reel." "Make this screen capture a vertical video." "Caption and brand this clip."

## The honest bit

Needs ffmpeg. It is the back half of the reel chain: the writing tools give you the script, this turns the recording of it into the finished post.

## What it does

You are not out of stories, you have just never collected them. This runs a guided interview that mines your own experience: the mistakes that taught you something, the calls you got right early, the questions people keep asking you, the 2am fix, the opinion your industry disagrees with. Out the other side: a numbered bank of up to 30 real stories, each with the detail, the stakes, the point it proves, and a ready first line for the post.

## How it works

Seven short rounds of questions, one at a time. You answer honestly, it digs once for the specific detail that makes each story land, and it banks the rows as you go. Nothing is invented: if you did not say it, it does not go in the bank.

## The Safe-Story Test

Every row gets checked before you see the final bank: would it be a problem if your boss read it, if a colleague screenshotted it, or if it touches anything a contract covers? Flagged rows are not banned, they get rewritten without the identifying details. Your day job funds the brand; nothing here should risk it.

## When to reach for it

"I have nothing to post about." "Mine my stories." "What should I post about?"

## The honest bit

It banks the stories and the post ideas; you still write the posts. The version that mines your voice as well as your stories, drafts every post in it, learns permanently from every correction, and guards the day job on every draft is The Ghostwriter, the full system this skill is one working piece of.
